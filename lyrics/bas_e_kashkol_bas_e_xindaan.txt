Bas e kashkol, bas e xinda, ta e faqer o hajat bas
awa zanim, dunya zaneer, ta mate bar, ijazat bas

E azmaish, e afat uf, mate maloom, ma halat uf
ma rubaru kosi ki hai, hasher barpa qiyamat bas

Luan sawze diko no boom, luan gherde diko no bom
faqat e lu sida sada, ma tasum sher muhabbat bas

Shabe hijraan mokore zit, poshiko sher khoshpa umeed
tu chuchi mo gherika det, awa oreem e darbat bas

Ta kya ziyaan, no boi e zan, ma pora boi briko armaan
ta mokhto e zina qabool, gti lalim e saghat bas

Faizan gumraah bti baghai, ishq a tabah bti baghai
pisa khur ish kya wasa nki, hoten mashkoor hidayat bas

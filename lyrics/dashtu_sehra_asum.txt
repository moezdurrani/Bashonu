dashto sehraa asum, thrushni armaan o sum
sarsaro gaano sum e dilasa
waqto raftar hanun, wa har ghuzar hanun
drust e jahaan o sum e dilasa


ta he mozgan ot dii, mozeen hardio bee
qaseer wa ka keleer, tu shahensha
deewana de rawen, mastana d rawen 
dosi girwano sum e dilasa

e bas bechan abas, ghamo khatan abas
e khamoshan abas, kaghba mashar
e di sukoon nki, hamish bosuun nki
gham e dawraan o sum, e dilasa

ta har sitam haasil, shramo jigar shereen
no re darman kyawat rahi marham
briyum jafaa no rem, no rem dawaa no rem
